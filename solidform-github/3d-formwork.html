Component 01

Phenolic Plywood Facing

The primary barrier holding the liquid concrete shape. Coated with a smooth, moisture-resistant film to ensure a high-quality architectural concrete finish upon stripping.

Component 02

Vertical & Horizontal Walers

A dual-axis support matrix. Vertical timbers distribute local panel loads directly into secondary horizontal double-steel channels to prevent flexing.

Component 03

High-Tensile Tie Rods & Nuts

The critical tension elements running straight through the wall core. These lock opposing forms together, resisting bursting forces from heavy concrete pours.
